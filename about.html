About Sentellect

Revolutionizing Mathematics Education Through Emotional Intelligence

Project Overview

Sentellect is an innovative Final Year Project that addresses a critical challenge in mathematics education: the emotional barriers that prevent students from achieving their full potential. Traditional e-learning platforms follow a one-size-fits-all approach, ignoring the crucial role that emotions play in learning effectiveness.

Our platform combines artificial intelligence with emotional awareness to create a truly personalized learning experience for HSSC students following the Punjab Textbook Board mathematics curriculum. By monitoring and responding to students' emotional states, Sentellect adapts content difficulty, pacing, and delivery methods to optimize learning outcomes.

The system goes beyond simple content delivery by providing explainable AI recommendations, helping students understand not just what to learn, but why certain topics are suggested and how the platform adapts to their needs.
