Concept Quiz

This quiz covers function composition, inverses, limits, and continuity from your chapter material.

Test Your Knowledge (10 Questions)

1. Given \( f(x) = 2x + 1 \) and \( g(x) = \frac{3}{x - 1} \), what is the composition \( g(f(x)) \)?

2. What is the inverse function, \( f^{-1}(x) \), of \( f(x) = 3x^3 + 7 \)?

3. How would you classify the function \( f(x) = x^3 + x \)?

4. What is the domain of the function \( g(x) = \sqrt{x^2 - 4} \)?

5. Evaluate the limit: \( \lim_{x\to 0} \frac{\sin 7x}{x} \)

6. What Cartesian equation is represented by the parametric equations \( x = a\cos\theta \) and \( y = b\sin\theta \)?

7. Which of the following is the correct identity for \( \text{sech}^2 x \)?

8. Evaluate the limit: \( \lim_{x\to-1} \frac{x^{3}-x}{x+1} \)

9. Given \( f(x) = x^3 - ax^2 + bx + 1 \), \( f(2) = -3 \) and \( f(-1) = 0 \). What are the values of \( a \) and \( b \)?

10. For what value of 'm' is the following function continuous at \( x = 3 \)?
\[ f(x) = \begin{cases} mx & \text{if } x < 3 \\ x^2 & \text{if } x \geq 3 \end{cases} \]
